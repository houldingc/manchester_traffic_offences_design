Make your plea: Traffic offences

Defendant's finances

For this type of offence the defendant will usually have to pay a fine:

• you must provide details of the defendant's income

• the magistrate will decide the fine based on the defendant's finances and the seriousness of the offence

• these details will be stored securely by the court
