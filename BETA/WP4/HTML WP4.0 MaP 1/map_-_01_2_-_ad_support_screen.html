Before you call:

A call centre is available for people who cannot use this online service without help. 

• have the pack we sent with you - you'll be asked to enter information from it

• be connected to www.makeaplea.justice.gov.uk from an internet-enabled device, for example, laptop, tablet or mobile phone

The call centre staff:

• will talk you through filling in this online service yourself

• do not work for the police or the court and will not be able to answer any questions about your case
