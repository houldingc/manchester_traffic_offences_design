What happens next?

1. There’s no need to come to court on the hearing date shown in the pack we sent you.

 

2. The court will tell you if you need to attend a trial, and what evidence you may

    need to send to the court in support of your case.

 

3. We’ll send you a letter with the court’s decisions within three working days after your hearing.

 

4. Quote your URN 11/AB/12345/12 if you contact the court about your case.

 

5. Print a copy of this confirmation of plea for your records.

 

6. If you requested email updates you'll be sent a copy of this confirmation of plea.
